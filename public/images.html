The Arr!(t) of the Image

I look good, don't I? That's right, you can put pictures into websites using HTML!

In order to begin, you need to put the image (of your choice) into your /images directory

Lets see some example code:

<img src="./images/edward-snowden-sxsw-lg.jpg" alt="My Self Portrait" style="width:650px;height:400px">

Here the tag <img> is used

The picture name includes the ./images in front of it. This is the path that the image is located, in this case the images directory.

The alt-"My Self Portrait" is the message displayed if for any reason the image doesn't load. An example alt is "Error: Picture not loaded"

The style of the image is the appearance of said image on the website. It defines the height and width of the picture (in px), and occasionally what type of borders it will have.

The result is a very BEAUTIFUL image of yours truly! (I know you agree too).
